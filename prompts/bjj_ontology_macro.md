# BJJ Ontology - Macro (Pass 1)

**18 Core Positions for Macro Analysis**

1. **guard** - Bottom athlete has opponent between legs
2. **mount** - Top athlete sits on opponent's torso
3. **side_control** - Top athlete chest-to-chest perpendicular
4. **back_control** - Top athlete behind with hooks
5. **north_south** - Top athlete head-to-head opposite
6. **knee_on_belly** - Top knee on opponent's belly/chest
7. **turtle** - Bottom athlete on hands/knees defensively
8. **half_guard** - One leg trapped between opponent's legs
9. **standing** - Both athletes on feet
10. **clinch** - Standing with grips established
11. **takedown** - Bringing opponent to ground
12. **single_leg** - Attacking one leg
13. **double_leg** - Attacking both legs
14. **passing_guard** - Moving past legs to dominant position
15. **sweep** - Bottom reversing to gain top
16. **submission_attempt** - Applying finishing technique
17. **scramble** - Chaotic transition, no clear control
18. **unknown** - Cannot identify position

**Usage:** Use these position labels for macro timeline. Sub-positions (closed_guard, spider_guard, etc.) can be added as sub_position field.
